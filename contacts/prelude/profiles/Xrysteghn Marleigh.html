Bio

Sergeant Alex Reynolds; codename Forge

Forge, a specimen of physical conditioning, possesses a wealth of knowledge in armaments and a commendable proficiency in piloting. His reputation as an arms smuggler is infamous, having orchestrated the clandestine transport of weaponry across interstellar boundaries, eluding the grasp of security drones for years and accruing a notorious record of damages in the millions. His capture was not a result of tactical failure but a conscious choice to preserve life over liberty.

Confined to his ancestral home in the verdant outskirts of what was once Havana, his father’s wealth shielded him from incarceration, liquidating assets to cover the astronomical fine. Yet, the gilded cage of house arrest could not quell his indomitable spirit. Seizing the opportunity, Forge secured a berth on the Asimov, yearning for the expanse of the cosmos over the confines of terrestrial boundaries.

His temperament, akin to the unpredictable nature of plasma propulsion, is both his strength and his vulnerability. With newfound autonomy aboard the Asimov, he dedicates his time to the art of drone navigation, offering his services to the Icarus Consortium and other entities in need of a pilot with his unique skill set. However, a digital specter shadows his every maneuver — a security subroutine embedded within his credentials ensures that the Icarus Ethics Committee remains apprised of his actions, a constant reminder of his checkered past.

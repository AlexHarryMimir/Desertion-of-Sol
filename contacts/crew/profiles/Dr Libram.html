Bio

Science Officer Dr. Elara Selene; codename Dr. Libram

Dr. Libram, a luminary in the field of genome research, played a pivotal role in the conceptualization and development of the Icarus Embryo Arc. Her expertise, now directed towards the Asimov’s monumental journey, involves oversight and communication with Icarus Mission Control. Distinct from the conventional command structure, she answers to an elite consortium of scientists, ensuring the mission’s scientific integrity remains uncompromised.

Her past achievements, though shrouded in secrecy, are etched into the annals of scientific advancement, her publications serving as a beacon for aspiring scholars. Despite her intellectual stature, Dr. Libram’s demeanor is disarmingly warm, yet her interactions often leave one with the impression of being a lab rat, as if she’s peeling back layers of a psychological enigma. Her humility belies her status as one of the preeminent minds of her generation, a testament to her dedication to the pursuit of knowledge.

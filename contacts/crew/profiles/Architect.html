Bio

Gargoyle Orion Blake; codename Architect

Architect could thrive in the underbelly of a metropolis, his dominion established through tendrils woven into the Nets. He is the quintessential product of adversity—a youth who clawed his way out of destitution. A scavenger of discarded technology, he carved out an existence within society under the guise of a mere username amidst the digital expanse. Dubbed ‘Architect’ for his adeptness in navigating the virtual realm, it was his innovative designs and prowess in unearthing and leveraging digital backdoors that catapulted him from the shadows of anonymity. His reputation as a masterful gargoyle was cemented within hacker circles, yet his identity remained shrouded in the Nets’ cloak of obscurity for years. His covert status might have remained intact had he not infiltrated a cargo freighter laden with Icarus’s pilfered secrets. The sale of this footage to Icarus unveiled their illicit experiments on human subjects. This revelation compelled Icarus to unmask Architect and seize the incriminating evidence before it could fall into rival hands. Discovered residing in Chicago, he was co-opted into Icarus’s ranks with a permanent appointment. Architect’s decision to embark with the Asimov crew was strategic, ensuring a life of uninterrupted comfort within the Nets—a sanctuary for the remainder of his days.

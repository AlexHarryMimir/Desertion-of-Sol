Bio

Engineer Tariq Novak; codename Vortex

Dr. Vortex, an alumnus of Oxford, boasts a distinguished PhD thesis on the effects of time dilation on entangled quantum states. His postdoctoral labor at the Icarus Particle Accelerator Labs in Austria further honed his engineering prowess. Driven by a personal quest to surpass the boundaries of human velocity, he eagerly embraced the mission aboard the Asimov, seeking firsthand experience of time dilation phenomena. While his academic pedigree instills a sense of superiority, often alienating him from those less versed in theoretical physics, his professional conduct is marked by an exacting precision and an analytical approach that eschews sentimentality in favor of empirical data.

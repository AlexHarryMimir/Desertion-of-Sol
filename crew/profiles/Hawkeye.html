Bio

Captain Leo Stratus; codename Hawkeye.

Hawkeye enforces a strict adherence to the hierarchical structure. His illustrious tenure with the Royal Air Force is punctuated by numerous commendations for valor and resilience, qualities that render him an exemplary leader aboard the Asimov. His role is pivotal during the critical phases of escape, acceleration, and departure from Sol’s gravitational embrace. As the emblematic representative of Juliette Bunk, Commander Hawkeye is vested with the singular privilege of accessing the main hull, subject to the concurrence of ‘Wildcard’, the Juliette bunk’s second in command.
